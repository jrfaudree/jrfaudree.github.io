	\item If $r \in \R,$ then $f(x)=\frac{x+r}{x^2 + r^3}$ is continuous on $\R.$\\
	\vfill
	\item If $f:\R \to \R$ has a horizontal asymptote, then at least one of the limits $\ds \lim_{x \to \infty} f(x)$ or $\ds \lim_{x \to -\infty} f(x)$ is defined.

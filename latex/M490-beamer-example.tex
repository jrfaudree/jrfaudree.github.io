\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Differential: Interpretation}

We found
\begin{center} the differential of $y=2x+\cos x$ \end{center}
was
\begin{center} $dy=(2-\sin x) dx$ \end{center}
and when $x=0$ and $dx=0.1$
\begin{center} $dy=0.2$ \end{center}

\vspace{2in}
\end{frame}

In a graph $G$, the average degree of a vertex is $\displaystyle{\frac{2 e(G)}{n(G)}}$ and hence $\displaystyle{\delta(G) \leq \frac{2 e(G)}{n(G)} \leq \Delta(G).}$\\
(Make sure to address both parts of this statement.)

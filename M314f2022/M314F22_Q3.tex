There are 10 points possible on this quiz. No aids (book, calculator, etc.)
are permitted.  {\bf Show all work for full credit.}

\begin{enumerate}
\item (5 points) Find the reduced row echelon form of the matrix below. Show your steps.\\

$\begin{bmatrix} 0&0&1&-4 \\ 0&2&-3&7\\2&8&4&2\end{bmatrix}$
\newpage
\vspace*{-3.8in}
\hspace*{-1.5in}\includegraphics[scale=1]{vector-space-def-book.pdf}

\vspace*{-3.8in}
\item (5  points) Let $k$ be a fixed real number and $V=\{f(x):\mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R} \: : \: f(x)=Ce^{kx}, \: C \in \mathbb{Z^+}\cup\{0\}\}$ under the usual function addition and scalar multiplication. (So, $V$ is the set of all real-valued functions of the form $f(x)=Ce^{kx}$ where $C \in \{0,1,2,3,4,...\}.$)
\begin{enumerate}
\item Show that $V$ satisfies (1) and (4) in the definition of a vector space.
\vfill
\item Show that $V$ is not a vector space.
\vspace{0.9in}
\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

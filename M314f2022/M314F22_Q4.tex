There are 10 points possible on this quiz. No aids (book, calculator, etc.)
are permitted.  {\bf Show all work for full credit.}

\begin{enumerate}
\item (5 points) Do the vectors $\{ 1+x, x-2x^2,x^2 \}$ span the vector space $\mathcal{P}_2,$ the vector space of all polynomials of degree 2 or less? Justify your answer.
\vfill 
\newpage
\item (5 points) Parametrize the subspace $W=\left\{ \
\bpm a&b\\c&d \epm \: : \: a+b+c=0 \text{ where }a,b,c,d \in \mathbb{R} \right\}$. Then express the subspace as a span.
\vfill
\textbf{1 point Extra Credit} Explain why, given your work in problem 2 above, you did not need to be told that $W$ is a subspace.
\vspace{1in}
\end{enumerate}
